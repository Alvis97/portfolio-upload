Hi! I´m Alva

I am a web developer originally from Sweden but currently living in London. I have a passion for both technology and art, and I have combined these two interests by creating this website. The purpose of this website is to showcase my artistic abilities and promote my work to a wider audience. Through my website, I aim to share my unique perspective and bring my art to life. Whether it's digital paintings, illustrations, or graphic design, my website showcases my creativity and my ability to bring ideas to life. If you are a fan of art and technology, I invite you to take a look around my website and discover my creations. I hope you enjoy what you see and that you'll come back for more in the future.

My art background

I have been drawing ever since I was a child, starting at the age of 2. It has always been my biggest hobby and I love to focus on being as realistic and detailed as possible. To further explore my creative qualities and practice new techniques, I attended Örebro Art School for a year. It was an amazing experience that allowed me to grow as an artist and develop my skills even further.
